# Civilization Layer

This layer extends the Human Experience OS toward civilization-scale calm infrastructure.

## Goal

Model how:

- movement load
- transport pressure
- crowd density
- emotional density
- recovery environments
- attention fragmentation

shape human wellbeing at neighborhood, city, and eventually planetary scale.

## Modules

### `backend/app/civilization/city_flow.py`
Models city pressure and stress propagation.

### `backend/app/civilization/attention_guard.py`
Protects human attention from digital fragmentation and interruption overload.

### `backend/app/civilization/emotional_geography.py`
Classifies places as calming, restorative, balanced, or overstimulating.

### `backend/app/civilization/collective_rhythm.py`
Tracks collective work/recovery/exploration balance and burnout accumulation risk.

### `backend/app/civilization/planetary_model.py`
Synthesizes city + rhythm signals into a long-horizon human experience state.

## Design Principle

The system should speak less as it becomes more capable.

If a calm outcome can be achieved without another interruption, the AI should stay quiet.
